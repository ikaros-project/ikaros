## Development Notes

This week moves Ikaros away from more manual module registration and toward modules being linked into the system through static initialization. The change strengthens the plugin/module architecture introduced in the public source tree by making module availability part of startup/link behavior rather than a separately maintained registration list.

The follow-up `Notify` formatting cleanup is small but practical. It improves the kernel's console output at a point when the repository is still being made ready for regular public development and build testing.
