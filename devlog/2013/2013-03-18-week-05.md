## Summary

This week increased the XML buffer size. Birger Johansson raised the buffer to 262144, giving the XML layer more room for larger configuration or network descriptions.

## Highlights

- Increased XML buffer size to 262144.

## Narrative

The week is a kernel-support adjustment for larger XML input. Ikaros relies heavily on XML/IKC-style configuration, and increasing the buffer size makes larger network descriptions less likely to hit parser limits. The change is small in history but important for practical use as models and examples grow.
