## Development Notes

The week improves the interactive development loop in the WebUI. Automatic restart and reconnect behavior made the browser interface more resilient when the runtime stopped or restarted. Restoring views after stop and completing persistent state for views meant users could keep their interface context instead of rebuilding it after each run.

The reconnect branch was merged after the persistent-view work stabilized. Follow-up commits removed debugging and comments, leaving the feature cleaner in the tree. The `Dictionary` type also gained `int` and `float` support, a small but useful addition for typed values moving through kernel and interface structures.
