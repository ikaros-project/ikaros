## Summary

This week completed a large vision-filter push and advanced the ANN trainer. Work recorded under `ikaros-project` added LoG and MedianFilter modules, sort and quick-sort support in `Ikaros_Math`, filter comments, trainer completion updates, row mean support, new average types, kernel size setters, and the ANN merge. Christian Balkenius added and completed ZeroCrossings work, added filter CMake files, made trainer/RBF commits, and corrected integer/long handling in sort.

## Narrative

The week is one of the strongest module-development weeks in the early repository history. The filter suite expanded from the previous week's `IntegralImage`, `GaussianFilter`, and `DoG` work into `LoG`, `MedianFilter`, and `ZeroCrossings`. Christian Balkenius drove the ZeroCrossings path through first commit, completion, and final filter touches, while work under `ikaros-project` added related math and filter infrastructure.

The math utilities grew to support the new modules. Sorting entered `Ikaros_Math`, quick sort was added, and int/long handling was corrected. Row mean support and new average modes broadened the numerical tools available to modules.

The ANN branch also moved from in-progress trainer work to a merged result. The trainer reached a minimal completed version, was updated, and then completed. Christian Balkenius added the first RBF commit before the ANN branch was merged. By the end of the week, both the image-processing and learning-module directions had advanced substantially.
